Common Health Issues

Pasty Butt

Dried droppings blocking the vent

Treatment:

Clean gently with warm, damp cloth

Spraddle Leg

Legs splaying outward

Treatment:

Correct early with a bandage "hobble"

Respiratory Issues

Sneezing, wheezing, nasal discharge

Treatment:

May require veterinary care

Coccidiosis

Bloody droppings

Treatment:

Requires medication

Pro Tips & Tricks

  • Line wooden brooders with aluminum foil for moisture barrier
  • Use red/blue nightlight instead of white for overnight
  • Place vinyl shelf liner under bedding for easy cleanup
  • Place marbles in shallow water dishes to prevent drowning
  • Use hardware cloth over open feeders to reduce waste
  • Add tablespoon of plain yogurt to feed once weekly (after week 2)
  • DIY electrolyte solution: 1 tsp sugar, 1/8 tsp salt, 1/8 tsp baking soda in 1 cup water
  • Add apple cider vinegar to water (1 tsp per gallon) twice weekly
  • Use shelf liner as brooder floor for first few days to prevent spraddle leg
  • Create small ramps or platforms to encourage exercise
  • After week 3, provide small container with fine sand for dust bathing
  • Play quiet radio music to reduce stress from sudden noises
